# Prospective PhD Students

I welcome applications from motivated students interested in pursuing doctoral research within these areas.

Successful applicants typically have backgrounds in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Human-Computer Interaction, Software Engineering, Data Science, Immersive Technologies, or related disciplines.

When contacting me, please include:

- A brief introduction and research interests
- An up-to-date CV
- Any relevant publications, software projects, portfolios, or technical experience
